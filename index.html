Whats New

  • New personal devices

    Personal devices are crusial for students to be able to access the internet whenever possible, which can help with learning and homework/tasks.

  • Upgraded school rules

    Students do stuff that teachers have not yet told them thats not right, so the school goes into shambles. Adding more rules is helpful because:
    1. Preventing other students from getting hurt. Many different actions younger kids can do can harm other students, which will lead to a bad reputation for the student and our school.
    2. Keeping the school clean. Many students throw toilet paper in the bathrooms while others put sticks in the drink taps. With these new rules, we are hoping that the above dont happen.
    3. Stopping them from getting in trouble in high school. Many students do not know this, but when they move into secendary, their actions can lead into detention and suspension. With these new rules, we are hoping that students know what is right and wrong, and dont land in these situations.
